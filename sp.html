Synchronized Pulses

Maya


Phones have become extensions of ourselves, we use and rely on them for everything. These small devices are our source of communication with the entire world. The day is filled with rings and vibrations trying to catch our attention and grab a hold of us. The notifications turn into an addiction, seeing our screens filled with notifications releases dopamine and we enter into a loop of waiting for the rings that make us happy. After a while, the bombardment of notifications becomes overwhelming and anxiety inducing. A single ring or ding! can send panic throughout the body. The ringing synchronises with ourselves in an inseparable way.
