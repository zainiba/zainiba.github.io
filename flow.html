Just Go With The Flow

Maya

Just Go with the Flow is a representation of my feelings surrounding the choices and decisions I encounter in my life. The way the model moves through the water resembles how I have spent a lot of my life going with the flow, listening and following every decision thrown my way. I have learned that this way of life has not changed, the fact that I can make my own decisions about certain things is almost a facade. Every choice I make is dependent on how my family would feel. Now I am making decisions based on what I want, but I still can't help but to think about what others will think and I feel the pressure pulling me under. This piece is a small reflection of my life and the fact that I am able to reflect on how I feel like is great progress.
