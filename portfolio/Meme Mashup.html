This new meme is a mashup of the Mona Lisa, and parts of Marilyn Monroe's face. I also added the eyeliner shape and the type of eyebrows many people spend hours grueling over to perfect. To me, this mashup looks as if Mona Lisa has a mask on, and her true self is hidden beneath. Based on the shape of her eyebrows and lips she looks like she is uncomfortable and she wears this mask as a shield.
