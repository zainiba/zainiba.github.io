Hello, Im Zay!

I am a New Media Artist pursuing a BFA in Digital Media Art at San Jose State University. I enjoy working in many different mediums, such as creative coding, net art, and 3D modeling. I particularly love fabricating sculptures and combining my new media skills with spatial art. I often explore new mediums and materials to conceptualize my experiences. Lately, I have been exploring the possibilities of Python and TensorFlow for machine learning, and Blender and Touchdesigner as well.
